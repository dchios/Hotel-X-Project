# Hotel-X-Project
**F&B Ops End-Of-Season Review**

Hotel F&B Department Dashboard – End-of-Season Review

**Overview**
This Power BI dashboard was developed for the Food & Beverage department of a hotel, designed to support end-of-season analysis and prepare for next-season operations. It consolidates data from multiple systems into a single, UX-optimized view that enables strategic decision-making across departments.

**Business Context**
The dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Review performance across board types (HB/AL)
- Analyze department revenue vs. warehouse imports
- Identify top-selling items and cost ratios
- Simulate menu engineering scenarios
- Current season performance
It’s part of a broader BI initiative to transition from static reporting to dynamic, day-to-day operational monitoring for the next season 

**Data Sources & Pipeline**
- PMS: Board type revenue (HB/AL)
- POS: Department-level revenue and item sales
- ERP: Warehouse imports and cost tracking
- Excel Files: Raw exports from systems
- Power Query: ETL process transform, clean, and load into Power BI

**Dashboard Features**
- One-page layout with intuitive UX/UI design
- Overview: Overall F&B performance ( visuals or switch to matrix view ) 
- KPIs: Dep.Revenue, Warehouse performance, F&B cost %
- Board Type Analysis: HB vs. AL revenue breakdown
- Warehouse vs. Department Revenue: Cost alignment
- Top N & % Analysis: High-impact items and ratios
- Menu Engineering Playground: Adjust menu price/items quantity parameters 
- Moving Averages: Trend smoothing for revenue
- Comparison: Current performance
